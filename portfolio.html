  • Modernizing the design while maintaining brand identity

Brand Identity Design

Project Overview

Evergreen, a sustainable home goods startup, needed a complete brand identity that would reflect their eco-friendly mission and appeal to environmentally conscious consumers. As a new company, they required a cohesive visual identity that would help them stand out in a competitive market.

The Challenge

The main challenges included:

  • Creating a distinctive brand identity in a crowded market
  • Developing a visual language that communicated sustainability without using clichés
  • Designing a flexible identity system that would work across various touchpoints
  • Establishing brand guidelines that could grow with the company

Our Approach

We began with an extensive research phase, analyzing market trends, competitor branding, and target audience preferences. We conducted workshops with the client to understand their core values, mission, and vision for the brand.

Based on our findings, we developed a brand concept centered around the idea of "mindful living." We created a modern, organic logo using a custom typeface, complemented by a sophisticated color palette of earthy tones with vibrant accents. The visual identity included custom illustrations, photography guidelines, packaging designs, and a comprehensive digital presence.

Results

The new brand identity received overwhelmingly positive feedback from both the client and target consumers. Evergreen successfully launched their product line with a cohesive brand experience across all touchpoints. The distinctive identity helped them secure partnerships with several premium retailers, and they reported 30% higher engagement on social media channels compared to industry benchmarks.

The brand guidelines we developed have provided a strong foundation for their marketing efforts, ensuring consistency as they continue to grow and expand their product line.

Mobile Banking App

We began with an extensive discovery phase, conducting user research with existing customers to understand their needs, pain points, and expectations. We created detailed user personas and journey maps to guide our design decisions.

Our UX team developed wireframes and interactive prototypes that were tested with real users. Based on feedback, we refined the design to create an intuitive interface that prioritized the most common banking tasks while making advanced features easily accessible.

For development, we used a cross-platform framework to ensure consistency across iOS and Android while still adhering to platform-specific design guidelines. We implemented robust security features including biometric authentication, encryption, and secure session management.

Restaurant Rebranding

Project Overview

Sapore, a family-owned Italian restaurant with 15 years of history, needed a complete rebrand to attract a younger demographic while retaining their loyal customer base. Despite their excellent food and service, their dated visual identity and marketing materials were holding them back in an increasingly competitive market.

The Challenge

The main challenges included:

  • Modernizing the brand while honoring its heritage and existing customer base
  • Creating a cohesive identity system that works across physical and digital touchpoints
  • Developing a distinctive visual language in a crowded restaurant market
  • Designing for multiple applications including signage, menus, packaging, and digital presence

Our Approach

We started with a deep dive into the restaurant's history, values, and unique selling propositions. We conducted interviews with the owners, staff, and long-time customers to understand what made Sapore special.

Our design team developed a brand concept that balanced tradition and modernity, with a refined logo that maintained elements of their original mark while introducing a more contemporary aesthetic. We created a warm, inviting color palette inspired by the Italian countryside, paired with elegant typography and custom illustrations of ingredients and Italian motifs.

The rebrand extended to all customer touchpoints including exterior signage, interior decor elements, menu design, staff uniforms, takeout packaging, website, and social media presence.

Results

The rebranding was launched with a special event that garnered local press coverage. Regular customers embraced the new look, while the restaurant saw a 35% increase in new customers in the first quarter after the rebrand. Social media engagement increased by 180%, with significantly more user-generated content as customers shared photos of their dining experience.

The cohesive brand identity has given Sapore a stronger market position and laid the foundation for potential expansion to new locations in the future.

  • Duration: 10 Weeks
  • Completed: February 2023
